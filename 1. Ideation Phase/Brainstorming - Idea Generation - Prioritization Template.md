# Brainstorming, Idea Generation & Prioritization

## Objective
To capture initial ideas, brainstorm features, and prioritize them for the "Strategic Product Placement Analysis" project.

## Idea Generation
* **Idea 1:** Create 8 diverse visualizations (bar charts, donuts, pie charts, bubble charts) to analyze average sales volume against product category, product position, and season.
* **Idea 2:** Build an interactive Tableau dashboard allowing filtering by Consumer Demographics, Product Category, and Product Position to empower managers to make data-driven decisions.
* **Idea 3:** Develop a 3-scene Tableau Storyboard to guide stakeholders through key insights: best performing categories, the impact of foot traffic on front-of-store sales, and optimal placement strategies.

## Prioritization Matrix (Impact vs. Effort)
| Feature / Idea | Impact (Low/Med/High) | Effort (Low/Med/High) | Priority |
|----------------|-----------------------|-----------------------|----------|
| Interactive Tableau Dashboard | High | High | 1 (Critical) |
| 8 Core Visualizations | High | Medium | 2 (High) |
| 3-Scene Storyboard | High | Medium | 3 (High) |

## Selected Ideas for Implementation
1. The comprehensive interactive Tableau dashboard containing 8 visualizations.
2. The 3-scene data-driven Storyboard highlighting key product placement insights.
