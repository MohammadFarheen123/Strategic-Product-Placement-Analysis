# Problem Statement Definition

## The Problem
Retail stores struggle to optimize product placement (Aisle, End-cap, Front of Store) without concrete data, leading to suboptimal sales performance and inefficient use of high-foot-traffic areas.

## Who is affected?
Store Managers, Merchandisers, and Retail Analysts who are responsible for product positioning and maximizing revenue per square foot.

## What is the impact?
Poor product placement decisions result in lower overall average sales volume, missed promotional opportunities, and a failure to capitalize on specific consumer demographics' buying habits. 

## Proposed Solution Concept
A Strategic Product Placement Analysis project utilizing a dataset from Kaggle to build a comprehensive, interactive Tableau Dashboard and Storyboard. This solution will visualize key metrics like Average Sales Volume by Product Category, Product Position, and Season, alongside Competitor Price analysis, enabling data-driven placement decisions.
