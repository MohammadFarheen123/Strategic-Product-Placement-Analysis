# Empathy Map Canvas

## User Persona: Retail Store Manager

### SAYS (What are some quotes or defining words from the user?)
- "I need to know exactly which product categories perform best at the front of the store."
- "Are our end-cap promotions actually driving higher sales volume compared to standard aisle placement?"

### THINKS (What might the user be thinking? What are their beliefs?)
- "Data-driven product placement is crucial to our efficiency and performance."
- "I believe clothing sells better when placed in high-foot-traffic areas, but I need data to prove it to corporate."

### DOES (What actions or behaviors did we notice?)
- Manually checks inventory and sales reports daily.
- Spends hours trying to cross-reference consumer demographics with seasonal sales spikes.
- Actively filters dashboard data by demographics, category, and position to find actionable insights.

### FEELS (What emotions might the user be experiencing?)
- Overwhelmed by raw data without visual context.
- Pressured to increase sales performance and efficiency.
- Confident when presenting insights backed by clear Tableau visualizations and storyboards.

### PAINS (What are their frustrations or challenges?)
- Lack of a centralized, interactive view comparing our prices to competitor prices.
- Difficulty communicating complex sales trends to stakeholders without clear visual storyboards.

### GAINS (What are their goals or needs?)
- A responsive, user-friendly dashboard providing actionable insights.
- The ability to quickly identify that 'Clothing at the front of the store has the highest average sales volume.'
