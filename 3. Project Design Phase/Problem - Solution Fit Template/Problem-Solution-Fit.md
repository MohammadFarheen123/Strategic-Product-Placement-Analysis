# Problem - Solution Fit

## Problem Identification
- **Lack of Visibility:** Retail managers lack a clear, visual understanding of how product categories perform in different store positions (Aisle, End-cap, Front of Store).
- **Inefficient Use of Space:** High-foot-traffic areas may be occupied by low-performing categories.
- **Unclear Promotional Impact:** It is difficult to quickly assess if promotions actually increase sales volume or justify price changes.

## Validating the Problem
- Historical sales data exists in CSV formats (like our Kaggle dataset) but is raw and difficult to interpret without data visualization tools.
- Decision-makers rely on intuition rather than data when planning seasonal store layouts.

## Solution Alignment
- **Targeted Insights:** The dashboard provides specific visual answers. For example, the grouped bar chart explicitly maps "Avg Sales Volume by Product Category by Product Position."
- **Actionability:** The 3-scene Tableau storyboard breaks down complex analysis into digestible recommendations, directly stating findings like "Clothing at the front of the store has the highest average sales volume."
- **Dynamic Exploration:** Interactive filters allow users to instantly test hypotheses about Consumer Demographics or specific Categories without writing queries.
